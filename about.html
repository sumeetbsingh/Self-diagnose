You should know...

The self-health diagnosis system is a diagnosis tool that will offer you a list of possible causes for your ills following a quick questionnaire. It then offers a range of different meditations that may help with any known cause. It also offers a range of home remedies that are often cheaper and more accessible than the prescribed medicine. it now possible to create systems which aims at providing a convenient way for people to diagnose themselves at the comfort of their home. We aim to create a web application that will give users an interface for the selection of symptoms that will generate the list of possible ailments the person may be suffering from. Along with the diseases, the website would also generate a brief description of the disease and possible prescription for those diseases. Our aim is to create a general interface that will help people understand their health status so that they do not ignore the signs of diseases that may be fatal in the long run. It is well known that many diseases can be cured if diagnosed at an early stage.
